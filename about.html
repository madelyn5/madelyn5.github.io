Bio

I am a junior at the University of Utah majoring in mathematics and minoring in computer science and biology. I am interested in algorithms and combinatorics, particularly with applications in biology. I have experience in both bioinformatics and algorithmic research. Upon the completion of my undergraduate degree, I plan to pursue a PhD and continue with research in academia.
