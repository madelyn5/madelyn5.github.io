I am an undergraduate at the University of Utah majoring in mathematics and minoring in computer science and biology. I expect to graduate in May 2027, after which I plan to pursue a graduate degree in computer science or mathematics and continue with research in academia.

My research interests include graph theory, computational complexity, parameterized algorithms, and bioinformatics. I am particularly interested in working on combinatorial problems and algorithm development motivated by biological applications.
